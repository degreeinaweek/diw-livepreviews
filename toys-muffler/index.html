The workshop

Half a century. One family. One workshop.

Toy's opened on the corner of Gomersall and Oxley in 1974, when Redcliffe was still all weatherboard cottages and the Hornibrook Bridge was the only way in. Three generations of the family have run it since. Robert is the one you'll meet today.

The model hasn't changed much. Tell you what's wrong, tell you what it'll cost, stand behind the work. People come back for the second car, then the third. Their kids bring their cars in. That's the whole business.

Same corner. Same family. Since 1974.

If you're new to the peninsula and looking for someone you can actually trust with the car, we'd be glad to meet you.
