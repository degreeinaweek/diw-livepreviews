Why grassroots

Independent on purpose.

Grassroots is one physio, one room, by design. Not a clinic with rotating staff. Not a chain with KPI dashboards. The same hands that take your first appointment are the ones that follow you through.

The shared site at Dolphins Fitness & Aquatic was intentional too — easy parking, no medical-foyer feeling, and the option to roll straight into a pool or gym session as part of rehab when it makes sense.

A clinic that's built around the patient rather than the schedule is harder to scale, which is the whole point.

If that's the kind of physio you've been looking for, we'd love to meet you.

Your visit

What the first session looks like.

i

Conversation, then a screen

Twenty-minute conversation about what brought you in, your history, what you've already tried. A movement screen to see what your body is actually doing. No treatment yet.

ii

A plan, written down

You leave with a clear picture: what's likely going on, how many sessions we're talking about, what to do between visits, and how we'll know it's working.

iii

Treatment that fits the day

From there, sessions are paced to where you are. Hands-on work, exercise prescription, education — in whatever combination is useful that day. Plans change as you do.
