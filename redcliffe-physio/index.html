Our story

Twenty-eight years on John Street.

Redcliffe Physiotherapy opened in 1998. Before that, Denis spent his early career as the head physiotherapist for the Canterbury Bankstown Rugby League Football Club — a role most physios would consider a peak.

He moved to Queensland with a different plan: to build a community practice. Not a sports-clinic. Not a multi-location chain. A single building on John Street where the same team would treat the same families across generations.

Most of our patients have been coming here for more than ten years. Some are the third generation of their family to walk through the door.

Twenty-eight years later, that's still the model. New patients welcome — we just won't pretend to be new.

Your visit

What to expect.

i.
A proper assessment

Initial appointments are forty-five minutes. History, examination, and a written plan you take home.

ii.
Treatment by the same physio

You're paired with the practitioner most suited to what's going on — and you stay with them through the plan.

iii.
Long-term care available

Some patients see us once. Others build in quarterly tune-ups. Either is welcome — and both are common.
