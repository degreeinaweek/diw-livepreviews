— Questions we get

Honest answers.

Are you really a mixed practice in 2026?
Yes — most have specialised one way or the other. We've been a working mixed practice since 1955 because Narangba is genuinely mixed: suburbia on one side of Mumford Road, paddocks on the other. We didn't want to leave either group behind.
Do you do after-hours emergencies?
Yes — one of our three vets is on-call every night and weekend. Call (07) 3886 7433 outside hours and follow the voicemail prompt. If the on-call vet is in surgery, we route you to our partner emergency clinic.
How much will my visit cost?
A standard consult is $89. We post a full fee schedule in reception and on this page (see above). For surgery or complex cases, we give you a written estimate the day before — no surprise bills at pickup.
Do you do farm callouts to Burpengary / Caboolture / Dakabin?
Yes — we travel anywhere in the Moreton Bay region. Travel is charged at $1.20/km past the first 15km from Narangba. If a few neighbours are booking the same day we'll usually split travel — ask us.
Pet insurance / payment plans?
We work with all major Australian pet insurers and can lodge gap-only claims on the spot. For uninsured surgery over $500, we offer interest-free payment plans through VetPay — just ask at the consult.
