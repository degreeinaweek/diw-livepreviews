An editorial chair on the bay.

Serendipity opened on the Newport marina in 2018. The brief then is the brief now — a small senior team, slow appointments, hair that lives in real life and photographs well.

No apprentices on your colour, no double-booked chairs, no upsells at the basin. The salon takes referrals seriously and most new clients arrive through a friend who already sits here.

"Slow is the point. We'd rather you book once a quarter and walk out with the right hair than three times a year and learn to live with it."

If you've moved to the peninsula recently and you've been driving into the city for editorial colour — you no longer have to.

The Serendipity team · Newport
Senior stylists · Colourists · Owners

Five senior chairs, one standard.

Serendipity is a small senior salon. Every stylist on the floor has at least eight years behind the chair, and the work that leaves the door reflects that.

We trained in Brisbane, Sydney and Melbourne salons through our twenties and brought the standards north when the marina opened in 2018. The salon has stayed small on purpose — five chairs, one front desk, no chains.

If you're new to the peninsula or you've been searching for senior-only colour without the city drive, you'll be at home in the chair.
