The Redcliffe chair, unhurried.

Gather and Glow opened on Sutton Street because Redcliffe was missing something. A real salon. Soft music, a real coffee, a stylist who looks at your hair before they touch it.

We're not anti-trend. We just don't push them onto people. The work is honest — colour that suits your skin, cuts that grow out gently, products that don't punish your scalp.

"You'll sit here for three hours and feel like you've had a morning off."

If you've moved to the peninsula or your last salon felt like a conveyor belt, this is a quieter chair.

The Gather and Glow team · Redcliffe
Owners · Stylists · Colourists

Two hands, one chair at a time.

Gather and Glow is run by a small team out of the Sutton Street shopfront in Redcliffe. We trained in Brisbane salons through our twenties and opened our own room because we wanted the pace to slow down.

Every appointment gets a 10-minute consult before the cape goes on. We won't talk you into a colour your skin won't love or a cut that needs a 90-minute morning routine. Honest hair, gently done.

99 five-and-four-star Google reviews and counting. Most clients come weekly through word of mouth — usually from a friend who's just moved to the area.
