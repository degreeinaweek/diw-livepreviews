— The salon on Queens Pde

14 Queens Parade. Small. Stylish. Booked.

Dogge Lane is a two-table salon in a sky-blue shopfront on the Brighton side of Queens Parade. We've been here since 2018. We take five dogs a day, max, and we do it slowly on purpose.

We're known for doodles, but we'll style anything with fur — corgis, schnauzers, westies, golden bois. Every dog leaves in a polka-dot bandana, sized to fit.

— The honest stuff

things people ask before they Book in.

Do you only do doodles?
No — we're known for doodles because Brighton has a million of them, but we groom every breed. Schnauzers, terriers, corgis, shih tzus, you name it. The pricing changes by coat, not breed.
Is the polka-dot bandana really included?
Yes. Every dog, every visit. Pink, teal, peach or grey — we pick based on what suits your dog. Bring back the old one and we'll swap for a fresh one, no charge.
How do I book?
Easiest way is online via MoeGo — pick a day, a slot, and your dog. We confirm within a few hours. Phone (07) 3269 5450 if you'd rather chat to a human.
What if my dog has been kicked out of another salon?
We hear that a lot. Tell us when you book — we'll schedule you for a longer, quieter slot the first time. Most dogs settle by visit two.
Cancellation policy?
Cancel up to 24 hours out — free. Inside 24 hours, half the service fee. Sick dog or sick owner, any notice — no charge, just text us.
