— Honest answers

Things people ask first.copied straight off the front desk pad.

Why are you closed Wednesdays?
It's our deep-clean and grooming-tools day. We sharpen blades, scrub the daycare lounge, and catch our breath. We're open every other weekday and Saturdays.
Can I just walk in for nails?
Yes — Tue, Thu, Fri between 10 and 2pm. $15. Saturdays we keep nails appointment-only because the salon's booked solid.
Do you take new daycare dogs?
Yes, but only after a free 30-minute meet-and-greet on a quiet weekday. We need to see how your dog handles the room before we commit to a daycare day. We won't take aggressive dogs — that's not a judgement, it's just not what this room is built for.
What's the Members' Board really?
A real wall in the salon with photos of every dog that's been here three times or more. It started as a joke in 2017. Now it has 120+ members. Your dog gets up there on visit 3, and stays as long as they're still coming.
Cancellation policy?
24 hours' notice for grooming — free to reschedule. Inside 24 hours, half the service fee. Sick dog or sick owner — text us, any notice, no charge.

Ready to get on the board?three grooms and your dog's photo joins the wall.

Phone is the fastest way — we answer between grooms. Mention this site and we'll squeeze you in this week.
