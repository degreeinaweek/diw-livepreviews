— Meet the groomer

Hello, I'm Shirley.

I run this studio out of a converted room at my home in Warner. I work with one dog at a time — never two — because I want to know your dog by the second visit, not the tenth.

Most of my regulars are dogs who didn't love a big salon. Senior dogs, anxious dogs, three-legged dogs, scared-of-clippers dogs. We go at your dog's pace, and we stop when they say stop.

Drop-off is at the gate. I'll text you a finished photo when they're ready. That's all there is.

— Before you book

things people ask first.

Where is the studio?
5 Pallas Parade, Warner QLD 4500 — a converted room at the back of my home. Address shared on booking. Drop-off is at the gate so you don't need to come inside. Five minutes off the Bruce Hwy.
Is it really just you?
Yes — every appointment is one dog and me, start to finish. I don't run parallel appointments and I don't have an assistant. It's slower, but it's why nervous dogs settle here.
My dog is nervous / senior / a rescue. Should I bring them?
Yes — these are most of my regulars. Ask about The Quiet Day: the same full groom, but split into two shorter visits with a rest in between. Same price as the standard full groom.
How do I book?
Easiest way is to text or call 0420 774 150, or email shirleydoggroomingstudio@gmail.com. I usually reply within an hour during the day.
Do you take cats?
Sorry — dogs only. I trained for years on dog coats specifically and I'd rather refer you to someone who works with cats properly.
