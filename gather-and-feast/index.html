— our why —

"We started Gather & Feast because Caboolture deserved a real food cafe — somewhere mum can sit with her coffee, the kids get fed something proper, and you don't need a translator to understand the menu."

Mel & the crewJames Street · Caboolture · est. 2018

Made here

Everything baked, dressed, pickled and poured in our tiny kitchen each morning.

02

Fairtrade

Organic, fairtrade coffee. Wholesalers we know by first name. Suppliers we visit, not just call.

Family-built

Run by Mel + the family + the locals who fold into the team. Bring your kids — we have crayons.

Real choice

Vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free aren't an afterthought — they're built into the menu, not bolted on.
