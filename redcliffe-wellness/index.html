Clinical Focus

Treatment for the way you actually move.

Three areas we focus on. Each session is designed around what you walked in for — not a generic adjustment plan.

01 — General

General chiropractic

Treatment for back pain, neck pain, and joint dysfunction. Designed for the everyday: desk workers, retirees, parents managing the daily load.

02 — Recovery

Sports & injury recovery

Support after sports injuries, workplace strains, and post-op rehab. Sessions tailored to where you are in recovery, not where a textbook says you should be.

03 — Daily

Posture & desk-worker care

Ongoing care for office workers and tradies dealing with shoulder, neck or lower-back issues that come with the way you spend your day.

About Dr Steve

A practitioner who shows up.

Dr Steve has been part of Redcliffe well beyond the walls of his clinic. He's a senior member of the Redcliffe SES — going on twelve years — and has volunteered as a Senior Rural Fire Fighter for decades. Off the clock, he's a father of five.

He holds two law degrees from the University of Queensland and studied a doctorate at Notre Dame. Before chiropractic, he was a coroner, a scout master, and an army cadet.

This isn't a clinic that opened last month with a slick brand. It's a practitioner who's been showing up for this community in different uniforms for thirty years.

Now you can book the chiropractic part of him too.

Your Visit

What the first few visits actually look like.

i
Assessment

Twenty-minute conversation about what brought you in. Movement screen. No treatment yet — we figure out what's going on first.

ii
Treatment plan

A clear path forward based on the assessment. How many sessions, what each one is for, what you can do between visits.

iii
Ongoing care

Some people come once. Others build in a monthly tune-up. Either is fine — the plan is built around your body, not a subscription.
