A small room with care.

Kira opened the studio because Redcliffe deserved a chair that didn't feel like an assembly line. Quiet music, a real coffee, time enough to do the work properly.

The studio takes one client at a time. That means longer appointments and fewer of them — but it also means the hair gets the attention it actually needs, and the colour gets the time to settle.

"You're not slotted between two other heads — for the hours you're here, the studio is yours."

If you've been driving into the city for a stylist who actually listens, this might be your closer chair.

Owner · Stylist · Colourist

One chair, your hair.

I trained in Brisbane salons through my early twenties, then took a long way around through luxury studio work before opening Kira Jane out of the Jetty Arcade in 2023.

Most of what I do is colour — soft balayage, gloss work, and the slow corrective work that makes regrowth disappear. The cuts are second nature by now. The extensions side is invitation-only for clients I've already coloured.

If you've moved to the peninsula recently and you're looking for a stylist who works the way the better Brisbane studios work, you'll be at home here.
