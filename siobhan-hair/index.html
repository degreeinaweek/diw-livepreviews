A chair, not a chain.

The brief is simple. You sit. Siobhan does the work. No upsells, no rushed double-bookings, no apprentice handover halfway through your foils.

Colour is mixed for your hair, not for the next person on the schedule. Cuts are dry-finished so what you walk out with is what you live with.

One chair, your hair.

I trained the long way around — apprentice years on the Brisbane northside, salon work through the early twenties, then a slow pivot into doing my own thing out of Newport Marketplace.

What I do best is lived-in colour. Soft regrowth, painted highlights, glosses that actually move. The cuts are second nature by now. The rest of the menu is short because I'd rather do five things well than fifteen on auto-pilot.

If you've moved to the peninsula recently or you've been trying to find a regular chair without driving into the city — this is the chair.
