— The honest answers

Things people ask before they drop one off.

Do you take every dog?
No. Every dog does a free 30-minute meet-and-greet first. If they're not comfortable in a group setting, we'll tell you straight up — we'd rather not take them than have a bad day for everyone.
What do I need to bring?
Lunch (we feed at noon-ish), their lead, their vaccination certificate (C5, current within 12 months), and that's it. Beds, toys and bowls we've got covered.
What hours can I drop off and pick up?
Drop-off any time between 7am and 9am. Pick-up between 3pm and 5pm. We're flexible if you're stuck in traffic — text us.
What's your cancellation policy?
Casual days — cancel up to 24 hours before, no charge. Weekly slots — give us a week's notice if you're pausing. Sick dog? Cancel anytime, no questions asked.
Where are you based?
Kallangur, in the Moreton Bay region. We're 4 minutes from Mango Hill, 8 from North Lakes, and we'll send you the exact address once a meet-and-greet is booked.
