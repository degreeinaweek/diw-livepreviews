One chair, your hair.

I trained on the northside through my twenties and opened Bella Eve on Anzac Avenue because Redcliffe was short on small, slow salons.

Most of my work is colour — soft balayage, foils with bond treatments, and tonal refreshes for clients who can't get to me as often as they'd like. Cuts are second nature now. Bridal is by referral, mostly.

If you've been driving into the city for a chair, give the salon a shot — it's worth one Tuesday morning.
