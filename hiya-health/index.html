About Hiya

A clinic that doesn't feel like one.

Hiya Health sits on Redcliffe Parade, a couple of blocks from the waterfront. The fitout was deliberate — plants, natural light, music low — because allied health works better when you don't dread walking in.

The bigger reason to be here is the team. Physio, podiatry, and exercise physiology share a hallway, so the conversation between practitioners happens daily. If your physio thinks your knee issue is partly a gait thing, the podiatrist's on the next door.

It's a multi-disciplinary clinic with the size of a corner shop — and that's by design, not by accident.

Drop in, call, or book online. New patients welcome across all three disciplines.
