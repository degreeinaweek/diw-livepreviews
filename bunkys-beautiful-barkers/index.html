★ Meet the groomer ★

hi, I'm Bijanka.

I started Bunky's because every salon I'd worked in ran four dogs at once, and I just wanted to do one really well. So I do one. One dog. One hour and a half. No background barking.

Most of my regulars are nervous, senior, or have been kicked out of a chain salon for being "too much". Almost all of them settle by their second visit.

I'm full. I know. That's why there's a waitlist — and yes, it really does move.

★ Before you ask ★

the honest questions Things people DM us.

How long is the waitlist really?
Right now, about 2–3 weeks. We do roughly 10 dogs a week and we don't double-book. Drop your details in the form above and we'll text you the moment a slot opens — first in, first served.
Why won't you just take more dogs?
Because the moment we'd add a second dog to the salon, we wouldn't be Bunky's. One dog at a time is the entire reason the reviews are what they are. It's a feature, not a bottleneck.
My dog has been kicked out of another salon. Can I bring them?
Yes — most of our regulars came to us after a bad experience somewhere busier. Please tell us when you join the waitlist so we can plan a slightly longer slot for their meet-and-greet.
Where exactly are you?
Deception Bay QLD. The exact address is shared once you're off the waitlist — we run out of our home and we keep that private until we know we're working together.
Cancellation policy?
Cancel up to 24 hours before, no charge. Inside 24 hours, half the service fee. Sick dog? No charge, ever, no questions asked. Just text us.
Do you do cats?
Sorry — dogs only. Cat coats are a different skill set and we'd rather refer you to someone who does it properly. Ask us, we know two good cat-specific groomers locally.
